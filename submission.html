Codalab Submission



Registration

We use CodaLab to host our competition. Please register in our CodaLab evaluation portal.

Submission

Our CodaLab evaluation portal accepts submissions for any subset of the tasks. However, to participate on our Few-Shot NER leaderboard, you will need to submit predictions for all the tasks. Submissions are required to follow a specific format as we showcase in our submission example. Before submission, you can also use our Evaluation code to check whether you have the correct format.
